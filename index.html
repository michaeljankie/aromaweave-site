Introduction

AromaWeave® stickers are engineered using premium cotton-rayon fiber, a material that integrates advanced scientific principles with the inherent properties of natural cellulose. This page provides a detailed examination of the material composition of these stickers, focusing on the scientific basis for their construction and performance. Designed for optimal functionality, the cotton-rayon fiber blend exemplifies a synergy between natural luxury and cutting-edge material science.

Material Composition: Premium Cotton-Rayon Fiber

What is Cotton-Rayon Fiber?

The stickers are crafted from a hybrid fiber composed of natural cotton and rayon, a regenerated cellulose material. This combination is deliberately selected to exploit the complementary attributes of each component:

  • Cotton: A natural polymer consisting of β-1,4-linked D-glucose units, cotton forms long, linear cellulose chains. These chains contribute to the fiber’s breathability, softness, and structural flexibility.
  • Rayon: Derived from wood pulp via the viscose process, rayon is a semi-synthetic fiber with a semi-crystalline structure. It enhances the blend’s durability, smoothness, and capacity for liquid retention.
  • Blend Characteristics: The cotton-rayon fiber in AromaWeave® stickers is formulated with a precise ratio to achieve a balance of strength, porosity, and absorbency. This creates a matrix ideally suited for retaining and releasing compounds, such as essential oils, with scientific precision.
